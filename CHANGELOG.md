## [1.0.0] — 2026-01-03

### Added

* First public release of OpenIPTVItaly
* Curated free-to-air Italian IPTV playlist
* Verified and publicly accessible streams only
* Complete and consistent metadata (`tvg-id`, `tvg-name`, `tvg-logo`, LCN)
* Category-based playlist organization
* Optimized and unified channel logos

### Notes

* This is the first stable public release.
* No DRM-protected or pay-TV content is included.
